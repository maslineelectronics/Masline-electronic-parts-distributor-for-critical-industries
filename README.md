## Quality Assurance and Verification

Quality is the foundation of Masline’s reputation as a leading **electronic parts distributor for critical industries**. We maintain a rigorous quality control system designed to detect and eliminate counterfeit or nonconforming components before they reach our customers.

### Our Quality Process:
1. **Incoming Inspection:** Verification of labeling, documentation, and part consistency.  
2. **Electrical Testing:** Validation of functionality and performance.  
3. **Visual and X-ray Inspection:** Detection of internal inconsistencies and defects.  
4. **ESD-Safe Handling:** Protection against electrostatic discharge during storage and transport.  
5. **Supplier Auditing:** Continuous evaluation of all vendors for compliance and reliability.

Each product is backed by a Certificate of Conformance and traceability documentation, ensuring complete transparency.
